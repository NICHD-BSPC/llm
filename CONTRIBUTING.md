Contributions are welcome. Please submit a pull request with a clear
description of, and rationale for, your changes.

The committing user is *personally accountable for* and takes *personal
ownership of* their code.

Disclose any use of AI tools and add to `docs/ai-disclosure.rst`.

If AI was used in generating code, it is fully expected that the submitter has
personally inspected and approved every line, and completely understands the
proposed changes. Any errors will be attributed to committing user, not to AI.
